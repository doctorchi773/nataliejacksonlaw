ABOUT

At Family Medical in Dunnellon, Florida, we are committed to providing our patients with the highest quality of comprehensive healthcare. Our team of experienced and compassionate providers is dedicated to meeting the needs of patients of all ages, from infants to seniors. We offer a wide range of services, including:

Preventive care, such as annual physicals and immunizations
Diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic illnesses
Management of chronic conditions, such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma
Women's health services, including prenatal care, well-woman exams, and contraception
Pediatric care, including well-child visits and immunizations
We also offer a variety of on-site services, such as laboratory testing and X-rays, to make it convenient for our patients to receive the care they need.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, and we are committed to providing our patients with affordable options. We accept most major insurance plans, and we offer a variety of financial assistance programs for patients who qualify.

We invite you to schedule an appointment with one of our providers today to learn more about how we can help you and your family achieve your health goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our patients with the highest quality of comprehensive healthcare in a compassionate and welcoming environment. We are committed to building lasting relationships with our patients and their families, and we strive to be their trusted partners in health.

Our Values

Compassion: We treat our patients with the same compassion and respect that we would treat our own loved ones.
Excellence: We are committed to providing our patients with the highest quality of care, and we continually strive to improve our practices.
Accessibility: We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, and we work to make our services affordable and convenient for our patients.
Partnership: We believe that the best healthcare is achieved when patients and providers work together. We collaborate with our patients to develop personalized care plans that meet their individual needs.
Our Team

Our team of providers and staff is dedicated to providing our patients with the best possible care. We are highly skilled and experienced, and we are passionate about our work. We are committed to staying up-to-date on the latest medical advances and best practices, so that we can provide our patients with the most effective and compassionate care.

Contact Us

To schedule an appointment or to learn more about our services, please contact us at (352) 484-0422. We look forward to hearing from you!
