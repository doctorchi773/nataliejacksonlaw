AttorneyX is a law firm located in Chicago, IL that provides legal services to individuals and businesses. This website policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

We collect personal information from you when you fill out a contact form, request a consultation, subscribe to our newsletter, or use other features on our website. The personal information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, location, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

We use your personal information to respond to your inquiries, provide you with legal services, send you marketing communications, improve our website, and comply with our legal obligations. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights and interests.

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze our website traffic, and provide you with relevant content and advertisements. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or by clicking on the cookie notice on our website.

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no website is completely secure and we cannot guarantee the absolute safety of your personal information. You are responsible for keeping your password and other login details confidential.

We may update this website policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice on our website or by email. Your continued use of our website after such notice constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

What information do we collect?
We collect the following types of information from you:

Personal information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address. We collect this information when you create an account, sign up for our newsletter, or make a purchase.
Non-personal information: This includes information about your computer and internet usage, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system. We collect this information automatically when you visit our website.
How do we use your information?

We use your personal information to provide you with the best possible service and experience on our website. This includes:

Completing transactions: We use your personal information to process your orders and deliver your purchases.
Communicating with you: We use your contact information to send you updates about our company, products, and services.
Personalizing your experience: We use your non-personal information to personalize your experience on our website. This includes things like recommending products to you that we think you might like.
How do we protect your information?

We take your privacy very seriously. We use a variety of security measures to protect your personal information, including:

Encryption: We encrypt your personal information when it is transmitted over the internet.
Physical security: We store your personal information in a secure location.
Access control: Only authorized personnel have access to your personal information.
How can you control your privacy settings?

You can control your privacy settings by logging into your account and changing your preferences. You can also opt-out of receiving marketing emails by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any marketing email.
